What is LeaveHome Safe?

  • mobile app
  • launched by the Hong Kong Government on November 16, 2020
  • LeaveHomeSafe

    • How LeaveHomeSafe Combats COVID-19 in Hong Kong?

    Protect the Community

    Record citizens' visits

    1. When entering a venue or geting in a taxi, citizens need to scan the specific QR code
    2. When citizens leave the venue or tasi, press the “Leave” button.
    3. Related record of the visit will be encrypted and stored in citizens' mobile device for 31 days
    4. The App will automatic display the QR code of vaccination record or Exemption Certificates
    5. show the Vaccine Pass to the premises’ QR Code Verification Scanner devices for checking and recording

      6. help health authorities trace close contacts of Covid-19 patients

      help to identify patients who make false claims about their whereabouts

      stop a potential community outbreak

    Example: LeaveHomeSafe in Schools

    • The Education Bureau
    1. Schools should set up QR codes for LeaveHomeSafe to facilitate all teaching and non-teaching staff, persons providing on-campus services and all other visitors (apart from those exempted) to use the mobile application each time they enter school premises by February 24 and thereafter
    2. Students entering school premises can be exempted
    • Hong Kong Baptist University
    1. Starting from 8 October, students are requested to scan the QR code using LeaveHomeSafe before entering the campus

    Protect Yourself

    Receive Exposure Notification

    1. Receive notification from the App if users were at the same venue at about the same time with a recently confirmed COVID-19 patient
    2. Notification will contain information on where and when the exposure might have occurred
    3. Health advice will also be sent to users
    4. To remind them to take proper precautionary measures, and seek medical treatment if need

    The Requirement of Using LeaveHomeSafe

    All customers entering catering premises and scheduled premises subject to the directions issued under Cap. 599F are required to scan the LeaveHomeSafe venue QR code using the LeaveHomeSafe

    • All catering premises providing dine-in services
    • Bars/Pubs
    • Places of public entertainment
    • Shopping malls
